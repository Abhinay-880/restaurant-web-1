Our Menu

Chicken Briyani

Aromatic basmati rice cooked to perfection with tender chicken pieces marinated in a blend of rich spices, saffron, and herbs. This classic Chicken Biryani offers a burst of flavor in every bite, served with raita and a side of tangy pickle.

Mutton Briyani

Succulent mutton chunks slow-cooked with fragrant basmati rice, infused with the robust flavors of traditional spices, mint, and caramelized onions. Our Mutton Biryani is a royal treat, bringing together the richness of mutton with the delicate aroma of saffron.

Paneer Briyani

A delightful vegetarian option, our Paneer Biryani features soft paneer cubes simmered in a medley of aromatic spices and basmati rice. This dish is a perfect blend of flavors, with each grain of rice soaking in the essence of saffron, making it a wholesome and satisfying meal.
